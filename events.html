Events

Join us for an exciting lineup of weekly events! Dive into the realm of Disney Lorcana with fellow Illumineers each Saturday at 2pm. Unleash your Pokémon prowess during Pokémon League also on Saturdays from 1-3pm. Immerse yourself in the digital world of Digimon during Open Play Wednesdays from 6-8pm. Set sail on a thrilling One Piece adventure during Open Play Thursdays from 6-9pm.


In addition to these regular events, we host special Trade Nights on occasion, providing a unique opportunity to expand your collection and connect with fellow enthusiasts. Unique Emporium loves to celebrate, from getting spooky for Halloween to our annual anniversary bash in August.


Our welcoming community embraces players of all ages and skill levels for an unforgettable gaming experience filled with friendly competition and a shared passion.


Be sure to keep an eye on our social channels for updates about our events! For event policies, please check our About page.
